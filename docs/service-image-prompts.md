# HiveFive Service Image Generation Prompts

## Image Specifications

**Dimensions:** 1200 x 800 px (3:2 landscape)
- ServiceCard thumbnail: renders at full-width x 144px tall, `object-fit: cover` (crops from center)
- ServiceDetail hero: renders at full-width x 320px tall, `object-fit: cover`, `rounded-xl`
- Keep subjects centered vertically since top/bottom edges get cropped on cards
- Wider compositions work best — avoid tall/narrow subjects

**Format:** PNG, quality 100%
**Color space:** sRGB

---

## Universal Quality Keywords

Include these in every prompt:

```
professional photography, sharp focus, high resolution, natural lighting,
university campus aesthetic, clean composition, vibrant colors, editorial quality,
well-lit, bokeh background, college-age subjects, modern setting, warm tones
```

## Universal Negative Prompts

Exclude these from every prompt:

```
blurry, low quality, pixelated, grainy, noisy, watermark, text overlay, logo,
oversaturated, underexposed, overexposed, distorted proportions, disfigured,
bad anatomy, extra limbs, cropped faces, artificial looking, stock photo watermark,
lens flare, motion blur, out of focus, poorly lit, dark shadows, cluttered background,
cartoon, illustration, 3d render, anime, painting style
```
